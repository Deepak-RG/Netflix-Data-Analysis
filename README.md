##  Project Overview
This project analyzes Netflix Movies and TV Shows dataset to uncover insights related to content distribution, trends, and viewer patterns using data analysis and visualization techniques.

---

##  Objectives
- Analyze distribution of Movies vs TV Shows  
- Identify top genres on Netflix  
- Study content growth over years  
- Visualize country-wise distribution  

---

##  Dataset
- Source: Kaggle  
- File: `netflix_titles.csv`  

---

##  Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas, NumPy)  
- Matplotlib & Seaborn  
- Tableau (Dashboard)  
- Jupyter Notebook  

---

##  Visualizations

###  Movies vs TV Shows
Shows percentage distribution of content types  

###  Content by Country
World map showing content production by country  

###  Release Trend
Line chart showing growth of Netflix content over time  

###  Top Genres
Bar chart showing most popular genres  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Movies dominate Netflix content  
- United States produces the highest number of titles  
- Significant growth in content after 2015  
- Drama and International genres are most common
